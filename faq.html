Frequently Asked Questions

What is Emo-Reco?

Emo-Reco is a Machine Learning powered tool that analyzes emotions from videos, providing insights into player reactions.

How does Emo-Reco work?

Users upload a video containing their gameplay and facial expressions. We processes the footage to detect emotions and generate a report.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We prioritize user privacy and do not store uploaded videos after processing.

What is stored?

Text entries containing emotion data!
Example entry: 24min 30sec - Anger

Can I share my emotion report?

Yes! Emo-Reco provides a sharing feature that allows you to share your results easily.
